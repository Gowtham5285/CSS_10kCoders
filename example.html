Why transitions?

Transitions help make UI feel responsive and natural. Prefer transitions on transforms, opacity, and filters for smoother performance.

Quick cheatsheet

  • transition: property duration timing-function;
  • Use transform for GPU-accelerated motion.
  • Avoid transitions on layout-triggering props (width/height) when possible.
  • Combine with @keyframes for repeating animations.
