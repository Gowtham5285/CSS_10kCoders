Subhas Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose, fondly known as Netaji, was one of India’s most courageous freedom fighters. His slogan “Jai Hind” became the battle cry of the Indian independence movement. 🇮🇳

Born on 23 January 1897 in Cuttack, Odisha, Netaji was a brilliant student and a visionary leader. He cleared the Indian Civil Services exam but resigned to serve his country’s struggle for freedom. ✊

He formed the Indian National Army (INA) to fight British colonial rule, believing that freedom could only be achieved through action. His leadership inspired countless Indians to rise with pride and patriotism. 💥

Even today, his famous words — “Give me blood, and I will give you freedom!” — echo in the hearts of millions who honor his courage and sacrifice. 🌟
