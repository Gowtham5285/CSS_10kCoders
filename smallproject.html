Hinata Hyuga

Hinata Hyuga is a kunoichi from the Hyuga Clan, one of the strongest clans in Konoha. She possesses the Byakugan, which grants her near 360Â° vision and precise chakra control. Known for her gentle and shy nature, Hinata always admired Naruto Uzumaki, drawing courage and strength from him. She becomes a skilled user of the Gentle Fist technique, striking chakra points with accuracy. Eventually, Hinata marries Naruto and becomes the loving mother of Boruto and Himawari.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa Ackerman is one of the main characters in Attack on Titan and a member of the Survey Corps. She is known for her incredible combat skills, strength, and intelligence on the battlefield. Mikasa deeply cares for Eren Yeager, protecting him at all costs after losing her family at a young age. Thanks to her Ackerman bloodline, she possesses enhanced physical abilities and instincts. She becomes one of humanitys strongest soldiers, feared by Titans and respected by her allies.

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and one of the main characters in Demon Slayer. After her family is slaughtered by demons, she is turned into a demon herself but retains her humanity. Unlike most demons, Nezuko does not harm humans and fights alongside Tanjiro to protect them. She has incredible regenerative powers, superhuman strength, and the unique ability to change her size. Despite being a demon, Nezuko shows kindness and compassion, becoming a symbol of hope in the series.
