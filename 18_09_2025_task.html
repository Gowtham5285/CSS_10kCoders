Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki is the main character of the anime and manga series Naruto by Masashi Kishimoto. He is a ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who carries the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed inside him. Despite being shunned as a child, he dreams of becoming Hokage, the strongest leader of his village. Known for his determination and optimism, he never gives up on his friends or his goals. His journey shows how an outcast boy grows into a respected hero who protects the entire ninja world.

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main character of the anime and manga series One Piece created by Eiichiro Oda. He is a cheerful and fearless pirate who dreams of becoming the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure, One Piece. After eating the Gum-Gum Fruit, his body gained rubber-like abilities, making him strong and unique in battle. Luffy is known for his carefree nature, endless determination, and loyalty to his friends. His journey follows the adventures of the Straw Hat Pirates as they explore the seas in search of freedom and dreams.

Sung Jinwoo

Sung Jinwoo is the main protagonist of the Korean web novel and manhwa Solo Leveling by Chugong. He begins as the weakest hunter, struggling to survive in dangerous dungeons where others look down on him. After a mysterious encounter with a “System,” he gains the ability to level up infinitely, becoming stronger with each battle. Known for his calm determination and unmatched growth, he rises from the bottom to become humanitys greatest weapon. His journey showcases resilience, power, and the transformation from underdog to the most feared hunter in the world.

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series Attack on Titan by Hajime Isayama. He starts as a passionate boy who dreams of freedom beyond the walls that protect humanity from Titans. After witnessing his mother death during a Titan attack, he vows to exterminate every Titan. Later, he discovers his ability to transform into a Titan, which makes him a key figure in humanity survival. Eren journey is marked by his determination, inner conflicts, and eventual transformation into a complex character fighting for freedom at any cost.

Kamado Tanjiro

Kamado Tanjiro is the main protagonist of the anime and manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba by Koyoharu Gotouge. He is a kind-hearted boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, with only his sister Nezuko surviving but turned into a demon. Despite his hardships, Tanjiro remains compassionate and determined to find a cure for Nezuko. He is skilled with the Water Breathing and Sun Breathing techniques, showing great strength and perseverance. His journey reflects his unshakable will, empathy, and dedication to protecting others.
